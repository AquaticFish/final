About

In the eerie twilight of a small, unfamiliar town, Checkout, a timid young girl, takes her first brave step into independence by venturing to the local grocery store alone. The flickering streetlights cast long shadows on the deserted streets as she approaches the quaint store with its flickering neon sign. As Checkout navigates the narrow aisles, strange occurrences start to unfold. The flickering lights overhead create unsettling shadows that seem to dance between the shelves, and the once-familiar products now bear cryptic labels. As she moves deeper into the store, the air becomes chillingly still, and a faint whispering can be heard, echoing through the empty corridors. Inexplicable sights and sounds intensify Checkout's unease items on the shelves shift on their own, and distant footsteps echo when no one is in sight. The store, once a haven for everyday essentials, transforms into a sinister labyrinth. Panic sets in as Checkout realizes she is not alone. Phantom figures lurk at the periphery of her vision, and distant whispers turn into menacing laughter. Desperation consumes Checkout as she attempts to flee, only to find the exit doors mysteriously locked. The once-familiar grocery store morphs into a malevolent entity, manipulating reality itself. The atmosphere thickens with each passing moment as Checkout struggles to discern whether the horrors are real or figments of her terrified imagination. "Whispers at Checkout" is a spine-chilling short film that explores the vulnerability of stepping into the unknown, where the mundane can transform into the macabre. As Checkout grapples with her worst fears, the boundaries between reality and nightmare blur, leaving the audience questioning the true nature of the unsettling events that unfold in the once-ordinary grocery store.
